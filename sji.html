Over de musical

A Modern Take on a Classic Tragedy

Our drama club presents a fresh interpretation of Shakespeare's timeless tale of star-crossed lovers. Set in a contemporary urban landscape, this production maintains the original language while bringing new relevance to the themes of love, conflict, and fate.

Directed by Mrs. Johnson with choreography by Mr. Smith, this production features innovative staging and original music composed by our talented students.
